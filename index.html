Tips & Tricks: Travel Smarter, Wherever You Are
RELATED HOBBIES



Local Currency is Key

Order local currency through your bank before you go. It saves time later and you avoid having to stress about entering the bathroom without money for the cleaning lady.

Visa or No Visa?

What are the requirements for entering the country? Do some research or you may have to beg for the final fifty dollars from other passengers who planned ahead.

Vaccinations

Are you going somewhere that has a history of malaria? Cholera? Yellow fever? Get vaccinated at your local Passport Health and carry that paperwork with you while abroad.

Passport Help

Do you have a passport to travel outside of your home country? And, even if you do, does it have enough blank pages to meet the requirements for the countries you're going to?

International Calls

Is your phone "unlocked" to use data and cell service while abroad? Talk to your phone service provider. A good rule of thumb - keep the phone off unless you need it.

Embassies and ATMs

Where is your country's embassy located in case things go south? What about ATM machines? Find these when you're at home, not when wandering around with no cash.

Apps to Have on Hand

Know what the weather will be? Can you contact an Uber? Do you know the language? All can be solved through apps. Just make sure there's enough space for pictures too!

Important Paperwork to Keep with You

Have a printed backup of your passport, credit cards that are going with you, your passport pictures, accomodations, and tickets. Phones can die, but paper cannot.

Avoid Credit Card Freeze Out

Have you contacted your bank and credit card companies to let them know you'll be out of country? If not, do so. They may think your cards have been stolen otherwise.
